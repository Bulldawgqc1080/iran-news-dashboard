# Contributing to ClearView

First — thank you. This project only works if people care enough to keep it accurate and honest.

---

## What You Can Contribute

- **New verified facts** — Events confirmed by multiple independent sources
- **New official statements** — Government/military claims that should be documented
- **New media framing examples** — Patterns in how outlets are covering (or not covering) the conflict
- **Timeline additions** — Key historical or current events with sources
- **Source ratings updates** — If a news outlet's ownership or editorial direction has changed
- **Translations** — Help make this available in Farsi, Arabic, French, or other languages
- **Bug fixes** — HTML/CSS/JS issues

---

## Standards for Submissions

### For Verified Facts
- Must be confirmed by **at least two independent sources** (not two outlets citing the same wire report)
- Preferred sources: UN documents, IAEA reports, official government press releases, court records
- Include the direct URL to your primary source
- Do not editorialize in the fact description — state what happened, not what it means

### For Official Statements
- Include the exact source: which government body, which official, which date
- Link to the original press release or transcript — not a media paraphrase
- Do not present a government claim as a verified fact

### For Media Framing
- Must be a documented pattern, not a one-off example
- Cite at least 2–3 examples from the outlet
- Be fair — apply the same standard to all sides

---

## What We Do NOT Accept

- Content that cannot be sourced
- Propaganda from any government — including the U.S. government framing presented as neutral fact
- Content designed to inflame rather than inform
- Personal attacks on individuals
- Sourcing that relies solely on state-controlled media (Iranian, U.S., or otherwise) without independent corroboration for factual claims

---

## How to Submit

1. **Fork** this repository
2. **Edit** `index.html` — add your content in the appropriate section with the correct label
3. **Open a Pull Request** with:
   - A clear title describing what you're adding
   - Your source link(s) in the PR description
   - The label you've assigned (Fact / Official Statement / Framing) and why

---

## Code of Conduct

- Be respectful. People disagree on this conflict — that's expected and welcome.
- Attack arguments, not people.
- If you believe something is wrong in the dashboard, open an issue and explain why with sources — don't just delete it.
- This is not a place for political advocacy. It's a place for clarity.

---

*Questions? Open an issue and we'll respond.*
